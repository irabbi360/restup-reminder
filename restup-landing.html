RestUp Reminder

A Break Timer App

Manage and Enforce Regular Breaks

RestUp Reminder is a desktop application designed to help manage and enforce regular breaks. It's available across Windows, macOS, and Linux platforms.

Features

  • Set your preferred break duration and frequency.
  • Choose between a simple notification or a fullscreen break reminder.
  • Personalize the messages displayed during breaks, shown randomly.
  • Customize app settings to choose break intervals, notification type (popup or fullscreen), and even popup window color.
  • Enable smart break tracking that resets the countdown if it detects inactivity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I see the app in the tray?

Some operating systems, such as Linux distributions running plain Gnome (e.g., Fedora) or Pantheon (e.g., Elementary OS), don't support system tray icons. In this case, simply re-run the app to open the settings window.

How can I hard reset the app's data?

In case the app is left in an unrecoverable state, you can reset the app data by exiting the app, deleting the below folder, and starting the app again:

  • Linux: ~/.config/restup_reminder/
  • macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/restup_reminder/
  • Windows: C:\Users\\AppData\Roaming\restup_reminder
